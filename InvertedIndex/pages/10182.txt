2024 NFL DraftBen CollinsBBC Sport journalistPublished19 April 2024Updated 24 April 2025NFL draft 2025Venue: Green Bay, Wisconsin Dates: Thursday, 24 April - Sunday, 26 April Start: 20:00 EDT (01:00 BST, Friday)Coverage: Live text commentary via the BBC Sport website and app (from 23:45 BST)The NFL features more than 2,200 players across the league's 32 teams and most are recruited via the NFL draft each April.Every year thousands of players in the US collegiate system become eligible for the draft but the total number of selections is limited to about 250.Although players who go undrafted can still be signed later, most future stars are picked up during the three-day event.But early draft picks can often flop in the NFL while some chosen late become greats - Tom Brady was selected 199th overall in 2000 yet went on to win a record seven Super Bowls.Which team gets the first pick?The draft is divided into seven rounds - the first taking place on a Thursday, rounds two and three on Friday and four to seven on Saturday.All 32 teams have one pick in each round, going in the reverse order of the previous season's standings, unless they have agreed trades.As it stands, no first-round picks have been traded for the 2025 draft so each team will have one pick in this year's first round.The Tennessee Titans had last season's worst record so they will go first with the Super Bowl winners, the Philadelphia Eagles, going last.Who will be number one pick in the 2025 NFL draft?Published6 days agoThe Draft 'unicorn' that NFL teams just can't figure outPublished3 MarchHow do trades affect the draft?Image source, Getty ImagesImage caption, Travis Kelce (left) was selected 63rd overall in 2013 and Patrick Mahomes (right) 10th in 2017, and they have helped the Chiefs win three Super Bowls in the past six yearsInstead of paying transfer fees, NFL teams trade future draft picks and/or current players to acquire players from other teams or move up in the draft.The Kansas City Chiefs, for example, traded away three picks in 2017 to climb into position to select current star Patrick Mahomes.In 2023, the Carolina Panthers traded up to take the first pick from the Chicago Bears and select quarterback Bryce Young.When Carolina finished with the worst record in the 2023 season, it meant Chicago had the first pick of last year's draft as well as the ninth, taking quarterback Caleb Williams first overall and wide receiver Rome Odunze shortly after.Teams may negotiate trades at any time before and during the draft.How many players get picked?Image source, Getty ImagesImage caption, Kansas City Chiefs receiver Xavier Worthy set a new record at the 2024 NFL Combine, running the 40-yard dash in 4.21 secondsThe number of players selected varies slightly each year and this year there will be 257 picks.NFL teams scout players at college and then assess them during the NFL Combine, a week-long event in February featuring physical and mental tests where teams can also interview prospects.Each college/university holds a Pro Day, where draft hopefuls have another chance to showcase their skills, and they may also visit teams for more tests and interviews.Then comes the draft. Each pick will not necessarily be the best player still available, they may be the player which that team feels best suits their needs.The total number of picks varies each year as teams are awarded compensatory picks for players they lost in free agency.They are also rewarded with picks for developing minority candidates for head coach/general manager positions, while teams may have to forfeit picks for breaching NFL rules.Related topicsAmerican FootballMore on this storyWhat's it like to go through the NFL draft?Published23 April 2020Brady & Purdy - inside two of the NFL's biggest draft stealsPublished24 April 2024Get American Football news sent straight to your phoneTop storiesLive. Reaction, analysis and your views as Liverpool crowned Premier League champions8865 viewing8.9k viewing'A moment 35 years in the making, this was Anfield's day of destiny'Published14 hours ago'Liverpool still need new blood' - where will Slot strengthen?Published4 hours agoElsewhere on the BBCOn the frontline with the London Ambulance ServiceGo behind the scenes of the Time Lord's adventuresYour first look at the final series of Man Like MobeenGary Lineker reflects on 25 years of Match of the DayElsewhere in Sport'Liverpool still need new blood' - where will Slot strengthen?Could Wrexham really reach the Premier League?Meet the heavyweight who teaches sign languageThe contenders to derail England's home World Cup'A moment 35 years in the making, this was Anfield's day of destiny'Eubank narrowly beats Benn - extended highlights.
