VigourouxPublished3 days agoSheehan has made no secret of his desire to manage Swansea on a permanent basis having taken temporary charge for a second time.He took 11 points from seven games at the helm following the departure of Michael Duff in December 2023, and has 23 points from 12 matches in his current spell.The Millwall loss wiped out Swansea's very faint play-off chances, but they remain well placed in 11th having had relegation concerns when Sheehan was placed in charge after a run of seven defeats in nine league matches saw Williams depart.Swansea finish the season with a home game against Oxford United – who guaranteed their Championship status for next season by beating Sunderland this weekend -
