The final takes place on 31 May at Allianz Arena in Munich.England secure a fifth Champions League spotEngland are top of the coefficient table and Arsenal's win over Real ensured the Premier League would get an extra spot in the Champions League.That means the top five in the Premier League will all play in next season's Champions League.
