"I'm excited to get straight to work with such a talented group of players," she said.Merricks will link up with women's technical director Hope Powell, who she worked with at Brighton as assistant manager and development coach. She also worked closely with Birmingham City assistant manager Emily Simpkins at both England Under-19s and Brighton.Merricks on 'changing perception' of female coachesCarter's departure comes off the back of a 3-1 defeat by Charlton Athletic which leaves Birmingham City seven points behind leaders Sunderland with three games remaining in England's second tier.
