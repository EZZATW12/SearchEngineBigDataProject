a record they hold alongside IndiaPublished9 January 2025Updated 2 March 2025Full squads list for the ICC Champions Trophy, which is being held in Pakistan and the United Arab Emirates from 19 February to 9 March 2025.ICC Men's Champions Trophy 2025 schedulePublished9 MarchPakistan (hosts)Mohammad Rizwan (capt), Babar Azam, Kamran Ghulam, Saud Shakeel, Tayyab Tahir, Faheem Ashraf, Khushdil Shah, Salman Ali Agha (vice-captain), Usman Khan, Abrar Ahmed, Haris Rauf, Mohammad Hasnain, Naseem Shah, Shaheen Shah Afridi, Imam-ul-Haq.Imam-ul-Haq replaced the injured Fakhar Zaman on 20 February.AfghanistanHashmatullah Shahidi (capt), Ibrahim Zadran, Rahmanullah Gurbaz, Sediqullah Atal, Rahmat Shah, Ikram Alikhil, Gulbadin Naib, Azmatullah Omarzai, Mohammad Nabi, Rashid Khan, Nangyal Kharot, Noor Ahmad, Fazalhaq Farooqi, Farid Malik, Naveed Zadran.Reserves: Darwish Rasooli, Bilal Sami.AustraliaSteve Smith (capt), Sean Abbott, Alex Carey, Cooper Connolly, Ben Dwarshuis, Nathan Ellis, Jake Fraser-McGurk, Aaron Hardie, Travis Head, Josh Inglis, Spencer Johnson, Marnus Labuschagne, Glenn Maxwell, Tanveer Sangha, Adam Zampa.Mitchell Starc opted out of the tournament on 12 February. Pat Cummins, Mitchell Marsh and Josh Hazlewood have been ruled out by injury, while Marcus Stoinis has retired from ODI cricket. All five were in Australia's provisional squad. Cooper Connolly replaced the injured Matthew Short on 2 March. BangladeshNazmul Hossain Shanto (capt), Soumya Sarkar, Tanzid Hasan, Tawhid Hridoy, Mushfiqur Rahim, Mahmudullah, Jaker Ali Anik, Mehidy Hasan Miraz, Rishad Hossain, Taskin Ahmed, Mustafizur Rahman, Parvez Hossai Emon, Nasum Ahmed, Tanzim Hasan Sakib, Nahid Rana.EnglandJos Buttler (capt), Rehan Ahmed, Jofra Archer, Gus Atkinson, Tom Banton, Harry Brook, Ben Duckett, Liam Livingstone, Saqib Mahmood, Jamie Overton, Adil Rashid, Joe Root, Phil Salt, Jamie Smith (wk), Mark Wood.Tom Banton replaced the injured Jacob Bethell on 12 February. Rehan Ahmed replaced the injured Brydon Carse on 24 February.IndiaRohit Sharma (capt), Shubman Gill, Virat Kohli, Shreyas Iyer, KL Rahul, Hardik Pandya, Axar Patel, Washington Sundar, Kuldeep Yadav, Mohammad Shami, Arshdeep Singh, Rishabh Pant, Ravindra Jadeja, Harshit Rana, Varun Chakaravarthy.Jasprit Bumrah was ruled out with a back injury and replaced by Harshit Rana on 11 February. Varun Chakaravarthy also replaced Yashavi Jaiswal on the same day. New ZealandMitchell Santner (capt), Michael Bracewell, Mark Chapman, Devon Conway, Matt Henry, Tom Latham, Daryl Mitchell, Will O'Rourke, Glenn Phillips, Rachin Ravindra, Jacob Duffy, Nathan Smith, Kane Williamson, Will Young, Kyle Jamieson.Jacob Duffy replaced the injured Ben Sears on 14 February. Kyle Jamieson replaced the injured Lockie Ferguson on 18 February.  South AfricaTemba Bavuma (capt), Tony de Zorzi, Marco Jansen, Heinrich Klaasen, Keshav Maharaj, Aiden Markram, David Miller, Wiaan Mulder, Lungi Ngidi, Kagiso Rabada, Ryan Rickelton, Tabraiz Shamsi, Tristan Stubbs, Rassie van der Dussen, Corbin Bosch. Corbin Bosch was named as a replacement for the injured Anrich Nortje on 9 February.
