ISO Language Codes
You should always include the 
lang attribute inside the 
<html> tag, to declare the language of the 
Web page. This is meant to assist search engines and browsers:

 <html lang="en">
 ...
 </html>

In XHTML, the language is declared inside the <html> tag as follows:

 <html xmlns="http://www.w3.org/1999/xhtml" lang="en" xml:lang="en">
 ...
 </html>
