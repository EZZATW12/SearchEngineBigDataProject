She said to me, 'never be afraid of youth, La Voix'."The Sussex Beacon, a Brighton-based charity which provides specialist care and support for people living with HIV, said Ms Trollette brought "immense joy to countless numbers of people within the LGBT community"."His compassion, generosity, kindness and razor-sharp wit will never be forgotten," a spokesperson said."Rest in peace David. Thank you so much, for everything."Follow BBC Sussex on Facebook, on X, and on Instagram.
