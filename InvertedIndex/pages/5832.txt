The new room will reduce waiting times for patients across mid and south Essex who need treatment that does not need to be carried out in a theatre, it added.Ayse Casey, deputy director of operations, trauma and orthopaedics at the trust, said: "The new procedure room will free up our main theatres for more complex surgeries, such as knee and hip replacements."Ms Casey added: "The room is a fantastic addition to Braintree Community Hospital, so patients coming to us for a simple procedure can be seen in a better environment."Those needing treatment for carpal tunnel syndrome, trigger finger and minor foot, elbow and upper limb issues could benefit from the room, said a spokesperson for Mid and South Essex NHS Foundation Trust.Follow Essex news on BBC Sounds, Facebook, Instagram
