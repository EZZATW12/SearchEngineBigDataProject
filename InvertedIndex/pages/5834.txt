"We recently introduced new heart monitoring equipment, improving our ability to gather and analyse data," Ms Broad said."AI is also becoming more integrated into our work, particularly in automating measurements and predicting patient outcomes. "It's exciting to see how these advancements will shape the future of cardiac care."According to the NHS Trust, there are 50,000 healthcare scientists across the UK working in research or treatment, and they are involved in 80% of all patient diagnoses.
