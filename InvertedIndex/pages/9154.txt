caption, Riccardo Orsolini came off the bench to score his 12th Serie A goal of the seasonSam DruryBBC Sport journalistSubstitute Riccardo Orsolini scored a stunning 94th-minute scissor kick as Bologna claimed a dramatic victory over Serie A leaders Inter Milan.In the last minute of added time, a long throw flicked off the head of an Inter defender and Orsolini was on hand to finish acrobatically at the far post.The third defeat in their past six away league games means Inter are level on points with second-placed Napoli with five games to play, but stay top courtesy of a superior goal difference.Simone Inzaghi's Champions League semi-finalists are still hoping to complete a Treble this season and face AC Milan in the second leg of their Coppa Italia semi-final on Wednesday.Meanwhile, Bologna are up to fourth as they battle to qualify for the Champions League for a second straight season.Victory was their seventh in their past eight Serie A home matches and
