It's such a special moment. It's great all the fans can celebrate together "Roy Boyes, who was at the 1989 Hillsborough disaster, added: "This means so much."The last time I saw them win was in 1990, so I've waited a long time. I was at Hillsborough so I have known the bad times but this is the happy time."EPAAbout 60,000 fans filled the stadium with many more watching across the cityFans sang, danced and let off flares as others perched on vehicles parked around Anfield.Retired decorator Sean Kelly, 72, of Tuebrook, Liverpool, said: "All our dreams have come true."This is such a special club and it feels amazing to have won our 20th title.
