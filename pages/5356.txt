Transport for London is trying to tackle this with a "chatty cabins" scheme, as part of its 25th anniversary, to get Londoners talking more to improve their mental health. They are invited to spark new conversations as they fly above the River Thames on the cable car between Greenwich Peninsula and the Royal Docks.Story by Gem O'Reilly & Vince RogersFollow BBC London on Facebook, X and Instagram.
